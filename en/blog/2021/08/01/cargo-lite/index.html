CARGO LITE

CARGO LITE represents a significant advancement in drone technology, designed specifically to meet diverse operational needs across various industries. As a multi-purposed multirotor drone, it combines versatility, efficiency, and cutting-edge features, making it ideal for a wide range of applications including cargo transport, surveying, agriculture, and emergency response. This drone is engineered to handle different payloads, adapt to various environments, and provide reliable performance in demanding situations.

Specification

Configuration : Quadcopter 
Flight Mode	   : Remote Control and Autonomous
Payload	   : 10-15 kg

Key Features of CARGO LITE

1- Versatile Payload Capacity

  • Adaptable Design: CARGO LITE is designed to accommodate various payloads, allowing it to transport goods, equipment, and tools ranging from lightweight parcels to heavier loads. This adaptability makes it suitable for logistics, agriculture, construction, and disaster relief operations.

  • Interchangeable Modules: The drone can be equipped with different modules or containers, enabling quick transitions between tasks. For instance, it can switch from a cargo transport configuration to a surveying setup with specialized cameras and sensors, maximizing operational efficiency.

2- Advanced Navigation and Control Systems

  • Autonomous Flight Capabilities: CARGO LITE features advanced autonomous flight capabilities, including GPS navigation, waypoint management, and real-time tracking. This allows for precise route planning and efficient operation without constant human intervention.

  • Obstacle Avoidance: Equipped with advanced sensors and computer vision technology, CARGO LITE can detect and avoid obstacles during flight. This ensures safer operations, particularly in complex environments like urban areas or forests.

3- Extended Flight Time and Range

  • High-Efficiency Propulsion System: CARGO LITE is powered by an efficient propulsion system that maximizes flight time, enabling it to cover larger distances without frequent recharges. This is particularly beneficial for long-range delivery and extensive surveying missions.

  • Battery Optimization: With an innovative battery management system, the drone can optimize power usage and extend its operational range, allowing for longer missions and reduced downtime.

4- Robust Build and Weather Resistance

  • Durable Construction: CARGO LITE is built from high-strength materials designed to withstand harsh environmental conditions. Its robust design makes it suitable for various weather conditions, enabling operations in rain, wind, or snow.

  • Water and Dust Resistance: With an IP-rated body, the drone is resistant to water and dust ingress, making it ideal for outdoor operations in challenging environments.

5- User-Friendly Interface and Remote Operation

  • Intuitive Control Systems: CARGO LITE comes equipped with a user-friendly interface, allowing operators to easily control and monitor the drone during flight. The interface provides real-time data on flight parameters, battery status, and payload conditions.

  • Remote Monitoring: The drone can be operated remotely, enabling operators to conduct missions from a safe distance. This is particularly advantageous for operations in hazardous areas, where human presence may pose risks.

About us

Drone Tech Company is focused on meeting Saudi Arabia's growing national defense and commercial needs by offering cost-effective, high-performance UAVs and drone services. By leveraging local expertise and reducing dependence on foreign technology, the company aims to position itself as a leader in the UAV industry. In addition to UAV design and manufacturing, Drone Tech Company provides a range of drone services, including aerial mapping, surveillance, and remote sensing, to support both defense and commercial sectors. These services, combined with cutting-edge UAV technology, enable the company to contribute to the global aerospace supply chain while enhancing national capabilities. The company's phased approach ensures that it can scale production efficiently while maintaining a focus on high-quality performance, competitive pricing, and local manufacturing.
